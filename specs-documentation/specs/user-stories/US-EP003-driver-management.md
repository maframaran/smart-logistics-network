# User Stories — EP-003 Driver Management

## Carrier Stories

**US-018** As a Carrier, I want to register my drivers with their license type and certifications so that they can be assigned to appropriate shipments.
_Priority: Must | Feature: F-010_

**US-019** As a Carrier, I want to suspend a driver so that they cannot be assigned shipments while under review.
_Priority: Must | Feature: F-011_

**US-020** As a Carrier, I want to see each driver's current status and daily driving hours so that I can manage compliance proactively.
_Priority: Must | Feature: F-012_

## Driver Stories

**US-021** As a Driver, I want to update my status to RESTING when I have completed my driving session so that the platform knows I am taking a mandatory break.
_Priority: Must | Feature: F-011_

**US-022** As a Driver, I want to be notified when I am approaching my daily driving hour limit so that I can plan my rest stop before being automatically restricted.
_Priority: Should | Feature: F-012_

## Platform Administrator Stories

**US-023** As a Platform Administrator, I want to reinstate a suspended driver to AVAILABLE status after a review is complete so that they can resume work.
_Priority: Must | Feature: F-011_

**US-024** As a Platform Administrator, I want to see all drivers who have reached or exceeded their daily driving hours so that I can monitor fleet compliance.
_Priority: Should | Feature: F-012_
